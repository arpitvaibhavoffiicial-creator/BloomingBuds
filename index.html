About Our School

A caring English medium school in the heart of Kahalgaon

The Blooming Buds Academy, located at Ramnagar, Kahalgaon, Bhagalpur (Bihar), nurtures children from LKG to Class VIII through an English medium, CBSE-oriented curriculum pattern. We are recognized by the Government of Bihar and focus on strong foundations, good values, discipline and joyful learning.
